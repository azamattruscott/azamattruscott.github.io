Places to stay

Accomodation in Stockholm

Stockholm is packed with places to stay; whether it’s for a single night or a whole week, there’s a vast array of accommodation to suit your style and preference. From modernised hotels, boutique styled Bed and Breakfasts to scenic Swedish campsites, you won’t fall short of any available options for your trip. Design on the frontline; Sweden’s popularised Scandinavian design, known for its minimalistic functionality and its simple presentation is resonated everywhere. Pay attention to the small details, for whichever accommodation you decide to go with, Scandinavian design will be tailored to suit the style of a place and amplify its dynamic through its functional presence. I guarantee that you will be inspired. What more can be said, browse around my shortlisted accommodations and see if any suit your needs. I’m sure you’ll find a place. Or two.

    Nature is inspiring. It allows one to unify themselves with the elements, become momentarily present with their location and with their mind. Ingeniously, nature may have perhaps inspired the entire nation of Scandinavia. Cold, densely packed forests inspired a design movement that not only had a regional impact, but also a global one. Open spaces, brightly coloured décor, functional design is all found within Swedish living spaces; and most certainly in your choice of accommodation. Wherever you choose to travel, in whichever place you choose to stay in, nature is implemented within the design of every area… unless you choose to camp out in the wilderness, you’ll practically get the whole outdoor experience. When it comes to design, functionality isn’t an independent expression sparked by a lingering thought, it’s more of an incorporation, a means to suit an entire population, a humble statement exclaiming its service to everyone, for every occasion.
