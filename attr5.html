Stockholm City Hall

ROYAL, GOLDEN DESIGN

The city hall, one of the greatest buildings in all of Stockholm; its lustrous architecture pioneers its own direction in Scandinavian design, and the municipality of the building, for it being used for governmental, official and even ceremonial purposes provides a grand interior for all who wonder between its walls.
