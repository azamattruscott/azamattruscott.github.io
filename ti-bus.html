Buses

Stockholm by bus

Traveling by bus in Stockholm would require you to pay for a ticket issued by the ‘SL’; these can be purchased in a variety of locations, including on the SL app and a payment via SMS.
SL Tickets have 3 main durations for how long they’re valid for – there is a ticket which can be valid for one day, one for 3 days and another for 30 days.
Note, if using the bus, you must always have an SL Ticket present, you can’t pay once you get onto the bus.
You can get fined if you don’t have a valid SL Ticket or travel on the bus without one. More information can be found on the visitstockholm website.
