Djurgården

A NATURAL WONDER

New York, Central Park; London, Hyde Park; Stockholm; Djurgården. This special area within the heart of Stockholm is adored by many – Djurgården, being the “Game Park” where King John III kept game, such as elk and deer roaming on the island. Being one of Stockholm’s most popular and most favoured attractions, Djurgården is an island packed with museums, places of entertainment and restaurants, also due to it being so vast and green, it’s rich with walking routes and hiking trails; be sure to spend the whole day here if you visit… and walking shoes, remember!
