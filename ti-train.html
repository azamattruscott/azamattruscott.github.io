Stockholm by rail

Stockholm's public transport is facilitated by "SL". An SL card can be purchased at an SL Centre and at railway stations and metro's.
Time validity for SL Cards are:

- 75 Minutes - Single Use Ticket valid on all public transport links for the specified time - price varies depending on where the ticket was bought.

- 24 Hours - SEK 155

- 72 Hours - SEK 310

- 7 Days - SEK 405

Note, above are regular fares for people between 20 and 65 years of age. A Discounted Fare is applicable for people below 20 years of age, above 65 years of age and Students with a valid student ID.
With these cards, an unlimited number of journeys can be made on any regular public transport network - buses, metro's, trams and some Djurgården ferries between Slussen and Djurgården, and commuter ferries 80 and 89. Note, an SL Card cannot be purchased on public transport.
