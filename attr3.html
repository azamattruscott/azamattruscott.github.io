Stortorget

OLD TOWN SQUARE

Ever walk down a narrow street, a two-way tunnel or over a small pathway? You may not think much of it, however, at its end is a marvel waiting to be seen. In the centre of Gamla Stan (the Old Town) is the grand Stortorget Square; housing the Swedish Stock Exchange, the Nobel Library and the Nobel Museum, the square is distinguished by the colourful buildings which sit Stortorget’s opening and its grand annual Christmas market. One of Sweden’s treasures, Stortorget shouldn’t be missed.
