Stortorget

OLD TOWN SQUARE

Ever walk down a narrow street, a two-way tunnel or over a small pathway? You may not think much of it, however, at its end is a marvel waiting to be seen. In the centre of Gamla Stan (the Old Town) is the grand Stortorget Square; housing the Swedish Stock Exchange, the Nobel Library and the Nobel Museum, the square is distinguished by the colourful buildings which sit Stortorget’s opening and its grand annual Christmas market. One of Sweden’s treasures, Stortorget shouldn’t be missed.

There is much to do in Stortorget Square – the Nobel Library and Museum are high recommendations, yet also admire the place for what it, a bustling, cultural marvel of a square found in the heart of Stockholm.

Stortorget is much more than an attraction, delve into Gamnla Stan and find yourself within the square, you’ll find it a great day out and definitely a thing to “tick off” during your visit to Stockholm.

Stortorget is found within the heart of Gamla Stan on the island Södermalm.
