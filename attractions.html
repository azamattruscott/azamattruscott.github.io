Top 10 Sights in Stockholm

Stockholm has many sights and attractions to offer. As a tourist, you can easily get lost in this magnificent town, and whatever you may stumble upon will carry its own story, its own mark, its own history. Unless you’re in a scurry, then by this matter, I urge you to ditch the map and completely lose yourself. From my past travels, I have condensed a list of attractions into Stockholm’s 10 of the most famous to visit during your stay. Now, I don’t know how long you’re staying for, however, just as a word of advice: relax, take it easy, most attractions may take up a whole day… make sure to wear shoes you’ll be comfortable to walk in. Also, most of the tourist destinations are close-by to central Stockholm; with restaurants, bars and café’s all offering a varied array of cuisines, you won’t be too far from food.

Stockholm is packed with tourist destinations. You may find you may not have time to take it all in. To see everything. This shouldn’t be a worry; there’s no particular way to travel. Within every place, a new perspective should be gained, a new culture embraced, a new cuisine to be tasted. Don’t overload yourself, don’t overcomplicate your plans; a place like Stockholm is admired for its minimalism and simplicity. These are the main elements needed to be taken away from any trip… with some hints of relaxation. Not only with Stockholm, but with all of Sweden, there’s an overarching contrast throughout – with tranquil forests and bustling cities, there’s something for everyone; you can find everything in some form or another… be daring, be curious… be willing to explore.

"...be willing to explore"

My father used to say that travel has a way of opening the mind. You’re no longer confined to your environment, you gain new perspectives through witnessing other ways of life. You develop a sense of maturity through appreciating cultures other than your own. You dare to leave home, only to find something greater than yourself, something outside the norm; your norm. In truth, I hope you discover that there isn’t a norm at all…
