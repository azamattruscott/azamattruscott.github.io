The 10 best hotels in Stockholm

Here’s my selection of some of the best hotels in Stockholm. Every hotel in Stockholm has its own style, chic and grandeur about itself; each one will amaze all types of minds, tastes and characters. Of course, Scandinavian design is within the core of the each hotel’s style, each to their own fitting a contemporary theme, promising the harmony of aestheticism and functionality. These hotels are all within the vicinity of central Stockholm, ensuring that you don’t have too far to travel to see the main attractions. Being centrally oriented, these places of accommodation will show you the great, bustling city of Stockholm. They’ll allow you to see the place for what it is; help you delve into the Swedish capital and resonate with its traditionally modern vibe. Keep exploring.
