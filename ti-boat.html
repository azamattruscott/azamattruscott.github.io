Stockholm by sea

The city of Stockholm is broken up into many islands, all interlocked and connected by nont only bridges, but boats and ferries. The sea transportation operating in Stockholm is operated under SL and you would need an SL Card to travel. The ferries grant you an opportunity to travel to the Djurgården and other points within the city. Ports from which you can travel from are all listed below.
Also, more information on the SL Cards can be found following this link: https://sl.se/en/fares--tickets/
