The best Swedish Campsites

Staying in the city is nice, however if you’re looking for a getaway which doesn’t involve busy streets, traffic and buildings on every block, then you should consider getting out into the luscious Swedish countryside. With richly green forests, scenic fields and plenty of wildlife, allow yourself to immerse in Sweden’s magnificent wilderness; find your tranquillity and let yourself switch off as you set your mind off the norms of busy life. Some of the best campsites allowing you to explore Sweden’s natural beauty are found outside Stockholm’s vicinity, yet not too far from the major cities.
