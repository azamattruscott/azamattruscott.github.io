Gamla Stan

SWEDEN'S OLD TOWN

Gamla Stan is one of Sweden's oldest places, it's the foundation from which Stockholm was built; you'd find a vast array of history between its walls - from shops and restuarants to narrow streets and the iconic Stortorget Square, Gamla Stan is rich with history and culture, a touristic gem there for you to explore.

Gamla Stan is a historical part of Stockholm, pretty much as touristy as it gets, or as you would want it to be. An attraction full of attractions within it, plan out what you want to see and be prepared for some walking. Also, as already mentioned, you don't want to miss Stortorget, the square is its own Swedish marvel you just have to see!
